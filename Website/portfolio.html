Solar System Python Project.


The program creates multiple planets of different sizes and colors that then proceed to orbit the sun one at a time.


Flappy Bird Project.


The program creates a circle sprite that will then move up and down. The player is meant to navigate the sprite through the two pillars that appear on screen. Each time the player passes the two pillars, an audio queue is played and the player's score increases by one. If a player hits a pillar than an end game screen appears which shows the player's total score and an audio sample is played to let the player know that the game is over.


Coin Run.


The program creates a moveable sprite for the player that must interact with coins before being able to unlock the exit. The player must avoid the enemy turtles that will send them back to the start. There is a timer and a counter that checks how many times the player has been caught and sent back to the start.


Snake.


The program creates a snake with a tail that increases in size as you eat bananas. If the snake touches the wall or the apples then it is game over. The player wins after surviving for 10 seconds and are shown their score.
